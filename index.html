About Me

I recently graduated from the University of California, Irvine, with a degree in Biological Sciences. Outside the classroom, I earned the rank of Eagle Scout with Troop 811 in Brea, CA, and continue to stay involved as a parishioner at St. Angela Merici Church.

I currently work as an EMT and am pursuing my goal of becoming a firefighter paramedic, a career that combines service, teamwork, and purpose.

I'm a multi-faceted person with interests spanning technology, science, and the culinary arts. This website brings together my professional experiences, community involvement, and creative interests. Here you'll find highlights of my Eagle Scout project, insights into my journey toward emergency medicine, and a growing recipe blog that reflects my love for cooking and experimentation.

When I'm not designing or working, I'll be in the kitchen developing recipes or outdoors exploring the beauty of nature.
