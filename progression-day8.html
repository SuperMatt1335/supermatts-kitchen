Day 8 Progress

The garden truly began to take shape during this workday. After weeks of preparation, clearing, and irrigation setup, our team focused on bringing life and color to the space. We planted two fruitless olive trees, established multiple themed garden sections, and continued refining the irrigation system to ensure proper water distribution. We also constructed the concrete base for the statue that will serve as the centerpiece of the garden. Accomplishments

  • Planted two fruitless olive trees as focal points of the garden.
  • Established a rose garden, succulent garden, and two butterfly gardens, adding diversity and color.
  • Completed most of the drip irrigation system, leaving final adjustments for the following week.
  • Constructed a cement base for the upcoming statue installation.

Challenges

The primary challenge during this stage was managing time and resources across multiple tasks. Coordinating planting, irrigation, and concrete work in a single day required careful sequencing to avoid damaging new plants or freshly poured concrete. The drip system also required detailed adjustments to balance water flow between the various plant zones. Despite these challenges, steady teamwork and communication ensured that each task was completed efficiently, setting the garden up for its final stages of development.
