Day 9: Final Touches

Adding finishing details and cleanup.

Day 9 Progress

As the project neared completion, our efforts turned toward polishing the site and ensuring every feature was ready for the final presentation. We cleaned up debris, adjusted the irrigation lines, and spread a fresh layer of mulch to unify the planting beds. Volunteers also helped position benches and finalize pathways to make the space inviting and ready for use by parishioners and visitors. The garden began to reflect the calm, welcoming atmosphere we envisioned from the start.

Accomplishments

  • Spread and leveled mulch throughout the garden to cover irrigation tubing and improve appearance.
  • Adjusted and tested irrigation zones to ensure consistent water flow across all garden sections.
  • Installed benches and trimmed plants, adding comfort and aesthetic balance.
  • Cleaned and organized tools and materials, leaving the site ready for the statue installation and dedication ceremony.

Reflection and Leadership Growth

This phase of the project offered a moment to step back and appreciate how far the team had come. What began as an overgrown, neglected lot had been transformed into a peaceful, purposeful space through months of collaboration and dedication. Coordinating volunteers, managing resources, and solving on-site challenges taught me valuable lessons in leadership, communication, and patience.
